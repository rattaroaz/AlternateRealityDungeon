CLOTHING & ARMOR SYSTEM
======================

This document outlines all clothing and armor available in Alternate Reality Dungeon. Clothing provides style and limited magical effects but no physical protection. Armor provides physical protection and may also have magical properties.

BODY PARTS
==========

The game features 6 body parts that can be equipped with clothing or armor:
- Head (helmets, hats, hoods)
- Hands (gloves, gauntlets)
- Arms (sleeves, bracers)
- Body (shirts, armor, robes)
- Legs (pants, greaves)
- Feet (boots, shoes)

MAGICAL EFFECTS
===============

Fire Resistance: Reduces fire damage by 50%
Cold Resistance: Reduces cold damage by 50%
Poison Resistance: Reduces poison damage and prevents poisoning
Regeneration: Slowly restores health over time
Speed Boost: Increases movement speed
Strength Boost: Increases physical damage and carrying capacity
Skill Boost: Improves accuracy and combat precision
Defense Boost: Increases physical defense
Stealth: Harder for enemies to detect you
Invisibility: Temporarily become invisible to enemies
Intelligence Boost: Increases spell power and learning speed
Fear Resistance: Immune to fear and intimidation effects
Experience Boost: Gain 25% more experience from combat
Mana Regeneration: Restores magical energy over time
Blood Rage: Deal extra damage when health is low

EQUIPMENT RULES
===============

- Only one item can be equipped per body part at a time
- Clothing provides 0 physical defense but may have magical effects
- Armor provides physical defense and may also have magical effects
- Magical effects stack if multiple items provide the same effect
- Items can be found in dungeons or purchased from merchants
- Equipment affects combat stats and special abilities
- Some magical effects are passive, others activate in specific situations

IMPLEMENTATION NOTES
===================

- Clothing items are stored in the clothingItems array
- Armor items are stored in the armorItems array
- Magical effects are tracked separately from physical stats
- Combat calculations include armor defense bonuses
- Magical effects are applied through various game mechanics
- Save/load functionality preserves all equipped items and effects
